Rules

1. Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines

⚠️ This is a catch-all rule. We base our rules on these guidelines. Violating these rules will lead to a permanent ban. We do allow client modifications such as BetterDiscord and Vencord. You are welcome to talk about them.

1.1. You must be at least 13 years old to be on Discord

You must meet the minimum age requirement for Discord within your country. Claiming to be younger, even as a joke, will result in a ban.

1.2. Do not use self-bots, nitro snipers, auto changing statuses, API exploits, or do anything that harms Discord’s servers

If we see that you are doing one of these, you will be banned for violating Discord's Terms of Service.

1.3. Do not engage or joke about organizing raids, doxxes, or swatting

Any threat or joke about raiding, doxxing, or swatting someone will result in a permanent ban.

1.4. Do not use Discord to scam or send malware / viruses / malicious links

Sending viruses or malware goes against Discord's Terms of Service, and we will ban you if we find out you have sent or participated in them.

1.5. Do not leak private information

We do not allow any identifiable information to be sent to the server. Giving out fake information as a joke may also result in a ban.

1.6. No Recording of Voice Channels

Recording VCs without consent is frowned upon in the server, therefore we do not allow it. Consent is needed from everyone in the VC.
